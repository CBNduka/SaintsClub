SAINTS CLUB

A onetime Subscription to a lifetime Event

1st Oct – 1st November, 2023

Asaba, Nigeria

The SAINTS-CLUB is an annual Federal initiative to scout for emerging footballers and Uniting Nigeria using technology-driven software development. The school is a place where students, Business investors, football and tech-lovers from different countries gather for short-term, project-oriented, project-driven education in Entertainment. Over the course of 30 days, each team of Federal University in the country would be selected from thier University leagues and featured on the Competition where winners gets signed on yearly contract basis in Europe to create and deliver a finished project.

This club is centered on a strong tradition of fostering the growth and success of young players, and this program represents the next step in that mission. By providing education and resources to promising footballers, the club hopes to help them achieve their full potential and make a positive impact on the world of football. The purpose of this football event is to raise funds for a religious club's program aimed at identifying and developing talented football intellectuals.

    The event includes the following types of activities:



  • Guest lectures and/or master classes on selected technologies.
    • Lectures are classes are provided by academic professors and industry experts. The main goal of each lecture is to give a good overview of the technologies; to help students to get a bigger picture of the particular domain, and to acquire a holistic comprehension of the problems, solutions, and challenges that exist.

  • Work on projects in joint international student teams.
    • Students will work within joint international teams (about 7 students per team) to develop small projects and apply their new domain knowledge to a particular sub-project. Each project is developed by the teams under supervised guidance.

Topic 2023

Smart context-sensitive traffic control

There are many tools in the software world that allow you to safely upgrade running systems without stopping them. Systems such as search engines, social networks, and streaming or multiplayer gaming platforms are almost never stopped when they are upgraded.

The event itself will be a high-profile, professionally organized football match featuring talented players from universities across the country. The goal is not only to generate excitement and interest in the game, but also to showcase the skills and potential of the players involved with investment plans. In addition to the match itself, the event will include a variety of activities and attractions designed to engage and entertain attendees. Proceeds from ticket sales and sponsorships will be used to support the players and the club's program. This may include providing resources such as training facilities and equipment, travel expenses, and support for players who are pursuing professional careers. The club also plans to use some of the funds to build structures for communities, such as football pitches and provide resources like road transportation and data connection. Overall, the goal of this football event is to raise awareness and support for the club's program, and to help nurture the next generation of football intellectuals. By bringing together talented players, passionate fans, and generous sponsors, the event aims to create a positive and lasting impact on the world of football.
